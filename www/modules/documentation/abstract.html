Workshop Abstract

This hands-on lab will lead you through a mix of self-paced and proctored exercises covering core and updated capabilities of Red Hat Enterprise Linux 9. Led by your favorite Red Hat field solution architects, key areas covered include:

  • Ease of use: web console, dnf

  • Security: Simple system-level disk encryption, session recording with tlog, net-filter and nft-tools

  • Performance: Tracing with ebpf, Cgroups v1 vs. v2, XFS enhancements

  • Management: Gold image creation with image builder, Containers with podman, skopio, buildah, boot image management with Boom.

And plenty more…​ There will be ample experts on hand to help and answer your questions. Join us and begin your journey to becoming a Red Hat Enterprise Linux 9 expert.
